## üìñ User Manual

### 1. The Concept
This isn't just a list of links. It's a **Deck of Opportunity**.
Each card represents a distinct "realm" or business model for earning money online. Your goal is to pick a a card that resonates with your skills and spirit, and "play" it until you unlock revenue.

### 2. The Deck (Dashboard)
When you launch the app, you are presented with the **Card Deck**.
- **Browse**: Scroll through 20 unique money-making paths (Freelance, SaaS, Crypto, Voice Acting, etc.).
- **Flip**: Click any card to flip it over. The back reveals:
  - Good for: Who this path suits.
  - Quick Info: 3 bullet points on how it works.
- **Select**: On the back of every card, click the **"PLAY THIS CARD"** button to enter that specific workspace.

### 3. Play Mode (The Workspace)
Once you select a card, you enter a focused "Play Mode" designed to help you execute.

#### üìú The Mission Panel
- **Objective**: Reads out exactly what you need to achieve in this realm.
- **Daily Quests**: A checklist of actionable steps to take *right now* (e.g., "Send 5 Cold Emails", "Research 1 Product").

#### ‚ö° Action Zone
- **Focus Timer**: A built-in Pomodoro timer (25 min work / 5 min rest) to keep you in the flow.
- **Scratchpad**: A temporary text area to jot down ideas, headlines, or to-do's while you work.

#### üéí The Armory
- **Tools**: Curated, direct links to the essential platforms for that specific business model.
  - *Example (Freelance)*: Links to Upwork, Fiverr, Gmail.
  - *Example (Crypto)*: Links to TradingView, Metamask, DefiLlama.
